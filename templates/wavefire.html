Precautions

If you are currently facing a cyclone, follow these steps to make it safer

  • Board up windows

    Helps to prevent further damage to the interior of your home, and will help prevent injury from broken glass

  • Turn off gas supply

    Gas lines can get broken and leak gas, which is a fire hazard

  • Stock up on essential electronics

    Stock up on batteries, battery powered flashlights, candles for light; And have a battery powered radio for information

  • Stock up on food

    Stock up on food and water and other non-perishable foods that can be prepared and eaten without having to be cooked.

  • Prepare a first aid kit

    Disinfectants, antiseptics, antibiotic creams, bandanges, cotton balls, aspirin, Tylenol, and a thermometer are some of the basics you need.

  • Secure all heavy and valuable furniture

    Helps reduce the amount of damage to it, and to helps prevent it from being thrown against your (or someone else's) home.
